T-SPIN

Introduction

A T-spin is a specialized maneuver where a T-shaped piece is rotated into a space that it could not simply drop into without the rotation. This move is a critical technique in competitive and advanced Tetris play due to the high damage it can output with only a few lines cleared.

A T-spin (Single / Double / Triple) sends (2 / 4 / 6) lines of garbage.

In other words, a T-spin Double deals as much damage as a Tetris with only half the number of lines cleared.

How to Perform a T-spin?

  1. Ensure your stack has the right shape to accommodate the T-Spin

  2. Drop the T-Tetrimino so it hovers just above the gap

  3. Use the rotation button to spin the T-piece into the gap.

  4. The piece should "snap" into position thanks to the rotation mechanics.
