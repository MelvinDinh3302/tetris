OPENERS

Introduction

Openers are pre-planned, often memorized, sequences of moves used at the start of a game to set up strong positions for line clears, combos, or attacks.

Some openers are designed to be aggressive, sending a significant amount of garbage at the start of the game. Other openers serve as a smooth transition into the midgame, setting up a strong foundation for continued play.

Flat Top TKI

TKI is an opener frequently used by professional Tetris players. It is known for establishing a clean structure early in the game, enabling a quick transition to the midgame. TKI opener is favored due to its simplicity and low-risk execution. It requires an early I-piece to set up.
