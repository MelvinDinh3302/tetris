MODERN TETRIS GAMES

Tetr.io

Tetr.io is a popular online Tetris game that offers a fast-paced and competitive environment for casual and pro players. It has a rank mode where players compete against each others and climb the leaderboard.

Platform: PC

Puyo Puyo Tetris

Puyo Puyo Tetris is a unique crossover game that combines the gameplay mechanics of two classic puzzle games: Puyo Puyo and Tetris. The professional scene is largely dominated by Japanese players.

Platform: PC, Switch, PlayStation 4, Xbox One

Tetris Effect: Connected

Tetris Effect is a modern, visually stunning and highly immersive version of the classic Tetris game. It has been praised for its calming and meditative qualities, while also providing moments of excitement and thrill.

Platform: Switch, PlayStation 4, PlayStation 5, Xbox One
