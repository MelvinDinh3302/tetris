6-3 STACKING

Introduction

The 6-3 stacking strategy in Tetris is a common technique used by advanced players to build a stable stack for efficient line clears, frequently used in versus or marathon modes.

Structure: The playfield is divided into two sections. A 6-column-wide area on one side. A 3-column-wide area on the other side.

Purpose: Faster than tranditional (9-0) stacking. Allow for t-spin setups.
